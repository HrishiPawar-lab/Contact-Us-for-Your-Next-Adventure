Interlaken Swiss Hotel Apartments

Interlaken is situated between two lakes, Lake Brienz and Lake Thun with their turquoise waters and beautiful mountain backdrops. The river Aare runs past the accommodation, and you can take the steamboats on the lake. There are shops, supermarkets, restaurants, entertainment all on your doorstep, and a 2-minute walk to the town centre.

Looking for accommodation near the train station? These serviced apartments are located 80 metres from the Interlaken Ost Train Station and 6 km from Harder Kulm in the centre of Interlaken. With to Swiss Hotel Apartments, the Apartments provide accommodation offering a balcony with views of the mountains.

Ski-to-door access, a ski pass sales point and ski storage space are all provided at to Swiss Hotel Apartments, and guests can go skiing in the surroundings. Zürich and Geneva are the nearest main airports, and the accommodation offers a paid airport shuttle service on request.

Engleberg Swiss Hotel Apartments

Engelberg is a picturesque mountain village in Central Switzerland, in the real heart of Switzerland. The village is surrounded by snow-covered mountain peaks, crystal-clear lakes, and lush meadows. Engelberg offers a unique combination of beautiful nature, elegant architecture, outstanding hospitality and year-round outdoor activities. In winter – thanks to its location in the most snow-sure area of the Alps – winter sports are available until late spring. In summer – blooming meadows throughout the entire region and a wide range of sport and cultural activities. Without question, Engelberg is also a paradise for hikers, climbers, bikers and golfers who want to enjoy their sports during the colourful autumn season.

These brand-new family friendly luxury serviced apartments, offer a magnificent view of picturesque Engelberg, just a short walk from the charming village centre. With 2, 3 and 4 bedrooms, furnished to a very high standard with designer items and fittings. The units are furnished with parquet floors and feature en-suite bathrooms, a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, assigned parking, spacious living room, an equipped kitchen, Italian rain showers, laundry machines and walk-in closets. Daily housekeeping service is offered, replenishing supplies.

All apartments feature panoramic mountain views which can be enjoyed from the large windows and also the numerous balconies and terraces.
